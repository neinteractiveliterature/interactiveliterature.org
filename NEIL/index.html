New England Interactive Literature

Welcome to New England Interactive Literature (NEIL), an organization all about Live Action Role Playing.

Our goal: Encouraging LARP (Live Action Role-Playing) throughout New England and beyond.

Ask ten LARPers in the area, and you'll get thirteen or fourteen different definitions of what LARP is. That's why we:

  • Run the New England Intercon convention, the premiere multi-genre all-LARP convention, that tries to attract as many different kinds of LARP as we can find. Hundreds of LARPers converge on Intercon every spring, to play in several of the dozens of LARPs on the schedule. We'd love to play in your LARPs, so propose running something. There are also panels, workshops and discussions on the first day of the con.

  • Run NELCO, the New England LARP Conference, with panels, workshops, and discussions about the theory and practice of LARP. NELCO is a weekend designed for LARP creators and GMs, to exchange ideas and experiences. Come teach us what you've learned!

  • Run Build Your Own Game (BYOG) seminars, an intense weekend-long effort to write and run a new LARP. Experienced writers and people who've never written a LARP before get together to produce interesting and successful LARPs.

  • Sponsor LARP writing contests like the Iron GM competitions and Small Game contests. Teams of writers sign up to produce new LARPs that are run at Intercon, for prizes and recognition.

  • Host a LARP Game Bank, with complete LARPs you can download and run. The Game Bank includes our contest LARPs and more. Submissions are also welcome. These LARPs are free, so take them and grow your LARP community!

  • Look for brilliant and clever new ideas about how we can grow LARPing and the LARP community, in New England and beyond. Tell us about it; if it's something NEIL can help with, there's a lot we can do to make the idea into reality.

Use the links on the left to learn more about who we are and what we do. Then come join us and tell us all about your LARPs!
