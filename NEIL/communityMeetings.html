NEIL Community Meetings

The next NEIL Community Meeting is scheduled for Sunday, March 6th. This meeting will be part of the Intercon P wrapup/Intercon Q kickoff meeting. The purpose of this Community Meeting is to elect three members of the NEIL Board of Directors.

The meeting will be held at the First Parish Unitarian Universalist of Arlington, 630 Massachusetts Ave, Arlington, Mass., in the Parlor on the Main Floor.

The NEIL Community Meeting portion of this meeting is scheduled for 4:30. The complete agenda and information is located on the Google Calendar event.
