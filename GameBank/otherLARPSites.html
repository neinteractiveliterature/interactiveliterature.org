Other LARP Sites

These sites are other places where you can download LARPs for your use.

Site Notes
Alleged Entertainment

Alleged Entertainment is a collection of talented LARP writers who are well-known at Intercon, the Festival of LARPs, and beyond. There are many LARPs here available for download, using several different LARP styles.
Interactivities Ink

"Hi. I'm Mike Young."

(Shouted by the entire room) "HI MIKE!"

Mike gets this response whenever he stands up in front of an Intercon crowd. Mike has written a lot of games, and he's very good at it. This is his site, which includes his LARPs, his non-LARP games, and some classic LARPs from several of his friends. Many of these have run at Intercon and many other places.
RPGnet Wiki

This site contains a diverse collection of LARPs. Most are free to download. A few LARPs cost money to download. There are several LARPs that have run at Intercon available through this site. There are many other LARPs that we know little to nothing about.
